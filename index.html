          Come relax and soak up the sun at beautiful Pleasure Island, North Carolina. Just south of Wilmington in New Hanover County, the barrier island, Pleasure Island is home to many happy islanders and whether you are looking for a weekend getaway, a longer vacation, or considering a move to the area, Pleasure Island is sure to make you feel at home.
