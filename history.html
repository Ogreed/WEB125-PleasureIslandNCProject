The History of Pleasure Island

How did the name Pleasure Island come about?

After several attempts at previous name changes, the Pleasure Island Chamber of Commerce finally landed upon the name Pleasure Island, around the 1970s.


Who was the first president of the Pleasure Island chamber of commerce?

The first president of the Pleasure Island chamber of commerce was Robert Cronly Plummer who served when the Pleasure Island chamber of commerce was formed in 1937.


How many years has the Pleasure Island chamber of commerce been around?

The Pleasure Island chamber of commerce is currently celebrating 75 years in total as an active group.
